# GitHub Trending – 2026-07-20 (Monday)

## Editor's Take
Two things genuinely stand out this week. First, **xai-org/grok-build** exploded to 20K+ stars in under a week after xAI open-sourced their full coding agent harness — a fullscreen Rust TUI that is a direct, functional competitor to Claude Code, with mouse interactivity and an extensibility story baked in from day one. This is the biggest coding-agent ecosystem moment since Anthropic shipped Claude Code, and it's rich content for a comparison or "state of the AI coding agent" piece. Second, from the monthly window, **langchain-ai/openwiki** from LangChain quietly shipped a CLI that auto-writes and maintains agent documentation for your codebase — a persistent pain point for anyone building or shipping agents. Pair these two and you have a full episode: the tools are evolving faster than the docs can keep up, and now the docs are agents too.
