## Editor's Take
Two repos stand out this week. **nexu-io/open-design** (2,186 stars, created just yesterday) is a local-first, open-source alternative to Anthropic's Claude Design, shipping 19 Skills and 71 design systems in a single TypeScript project with BYOK support — it's a direct counter-move to Anthropic's own design tooling and a ready-made topic for anyone covering the Claude ecosystem. **alash3al/stash** is quieter but equally interesting: a self-hosted single-binary persistent memory layer for AI agents, backed by Postgres with an MCP server built in — exactly the kind of infrastructure primitive that agent builders are hunting for and that makes for concrete, practical content about real-world agent deployment.
