# GitHub Trending – 2026-05-25 (Monday)

## Editor's Take
Two repos stand out this week. **open-gsd/get-shit-done-redux** directly solves one of the most-discussed problems in AI coding: context rot—the quality decay as a session fills its context window. It distributes work across fresh subagent contexts while keeping specs and roadmaps as persistent artifacts, and it supports Claude Code, Cursor, Windsurf, and others; this is exactly the kind of meta-workflow content your audience craves. Meanwhile, **MoonshotAI/kimi-code** is MoonshotAI's answer to Claude Code—a terminal-based AI coding agent that reads/edits code, runs shell commands, and searches files, making it the most direct competitive entrant to Claude Code yet from a major Chinese AI lab and a natural comparison piece. From the 30-day list, **nexu-io/open-design** (52k stars) is hard to ignore—it's a local-first, open-source Claude Design alternative that runs on Claude Code, Codex, Cursor, and more, already at a star count that signals real developer adoption.
