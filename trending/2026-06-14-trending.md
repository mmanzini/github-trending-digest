# GitHub Trending – 2026-06-14 (Sunday)

## Editor's Take
Two repos this week are worth your full attention. **DietrichGebert/ponytail** is a Claude Code plugin that enforces YAGNI (You Aren't Gonna Need It) at the agent level — it's a sharp, opinionated take on a real problem with AI coding agents: they over-engineer. The fact that it hit 7,758 stars in under 48 hours signals this resonates widely, making it excellent content fodder for a "how to make AI agents write less code" angle. **omnigent-ai/omnigent** is more ambitious: a meta-harness that provides a single interface over Claude Code, Codex, and custom agents, with policy controls and real-time collaboration on live sessions — the kind of infrastructure play that hints at where agentic developer tooling is heading.
