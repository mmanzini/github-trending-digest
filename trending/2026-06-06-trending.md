# GitHub Trending – 2026-06-06 (Saturday)

## Editor's Take
Two repos stand out today. **odysseus** is the obvious lead story: 58k+ stars in under a week for a self-hosted AI workspace is a signal your audience won't want to miss — it supports local and API-backed LLMs, ships with agentic tool-use (shell, files, web research), persistent memory via vector embeddings, and a deep-research mode, making it a credible open-source rival to Claude and ChatGPT for privacy-first teams. Right behind it, **memory-os** directly addresses one of the most-discussed design problems in agent architecture: how to give agents a coherent, layered memory across sessions. Its seven-layer approach on top of Qdrant is a concrete technical artifact worth breaking down for an agent-builder audience.
