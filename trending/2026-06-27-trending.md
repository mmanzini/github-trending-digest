# GitHub Trending – 2026-06-27 (Saturday)

## Editor's Take
Two repos this week are worth the creator's attention. **`deepseek-ai/DeepSpec`** is DeepSeek's freshly published full-stack codebase for training and benchmarking speculative decoding algorithms — it landed yesterday and already has 1,200+ stars, which signals real practitioner demand for open inference-optimization tooling; content angle: why speculative decoding is the lever LLM ops teams are pulling to cut latency and cost. **`QwenLM/Qwen-AgentWorld`** is the more conceptually novel one: rather than another agent framework, it's a *language world model* — trained on 10M+ real agent trajectories to simulate environments (MCP, shell, browser, Android, SWE) so agents can do RL without touching live systems. The zero-shot generalization angle is compelling and underexplored in creator content.
