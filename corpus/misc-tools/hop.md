# Hop

An open-source cross-platform desktop app for viewing and editing HWP/HWPX files — the dominant Korean word-processor format — on macOS, Windows, and Linux.

- **Repo:** [golbin/hop](https://github.com/golbin/hop)
- **Stars:** ~948 (2026-04-24, 5 days after launch)
- **Created:** 2026-04-19
- **Language:** TypeScript
- **Topics:** none

## Why It Matters
- HWP (Hangul Word Processor) is the de facto document standard in Korean government and enterprise; the official app is Windows-only and proprietary.
- Cross-platform open-source alternative fills a gap that has existed for decades — high demand in the Korean developer community.
- TypeScript / Electron stack makes contributions accessible.

## Key Takeaways
- Niche but underserved market: strong organic traction from a community with no good alternatives.
- HWPX (the newer XML-based format) is more tractable for open-source implementation than legacy binary HWP.
- No AI angle — pure document tooling, trending on community need alone.
