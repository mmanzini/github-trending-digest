## Why It Matters
- "Foundation model" framing applied to 3D scene reconstruction signals the same generalisation push happening in vision and language.
- **Feed-forward** = one inference pass for a scene, instead of iterative optimisation (e.g. NeRF-style training per scene). Implies real-time or near-real-time use.
- **Streaming input** = applies to robotics, AR/VR, drone telemetry — wherever data arrives over time.
